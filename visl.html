VISL™ — Vitium Intelligent Safety Layer

The world’s first intelligent safety layer for thermal, moisture, vibration and structural sensing.

VISL™ is a patent‑pending multi‑modal sensing material designed for caravans, marine, medical, battery logistics, Electric Vehicle (EV) packs, Ministry of Defence (MOD) vehicles, aerospace, infrastructure and space systems.

What VISL™ Is

VISL™ is a passive, sector‑agnostic sensing layer that detects early‑stage failure conditions long before they become operational hazards. It integrates seamlessly with the VISL Monitoring Unit (VMU) and the Vitium Analytics Platform.

VISL Sensing Modes

  • Thermal sensing — early‑fire and runaway detection
  • Moisture sensing — ingress, leaks, humidity
  • Vibration sensing — fatigue, instability, imbalance
  • Pressure / tension sensing — deformation, load stress
  • Structural sensing — cracks, delamination, impact

VISL‑SLEEVE™ — Underground Pipe Safety Sleeve

VISL‑SLEEVE™ is a patent‑pending sensing sleeve designed for underground water pipes, providing early detection of moisture ingress, thermal anomalies, vibration, deformation and structural stress.

VISL‑SLEEVE™ integrates with the VISL Monitoring Unit (VMU) and the Vitium Analytics Platform, enabling alerts, trend analysis and predictive safety insights for buried assets.

  • Moisture sensing — leak, ingress, saturation
  • Thermal sensing — freeze, overheating, abnormal gradients
  • Vibration sensing — instability, external disturbance
  • Structural sensing — deformation, stress, impact

VISL‑SLEEVE™ is suitable for water mains, service pipes, distribution pipes, deep urban assets, river crossings and critical buried infrastructure.

VISL‑MARINE™ — Marine Sensing and Acoustic‑Dampening Layer

VISL‑MARINE™ is a patent‑pending multi‑modal sensing and acoustic‑dampening layer designed for marine vessels and underwater assets, including submarines. It provides early detection of moisture ingress, thermal anomalies, vibration, structural stress and impact events while simultaneously reducing noise transmission and hull resonance.

VISL‑MARINE™ is engineered for hull interiors, bulkheads, engine bays, battery compartments, cabin structures and underwater housings. It combines VISL™ sensing capabilities with advanced acoustic dampening to reduce mechanical noise, vibration and resonance in demanding marine environments.

  • Moisture sensing — ingress, leaks, condensation
  • Thermal sensing — overheating, cold‑shock, abnormal gradients
  • Vibration sensing — instability, engine imbalance, hull resonance
  • Structural sensing — impact, stress, deformation
  • Acoustic dampening — noise reduction, vibration isolation, resonance control

VISL‑MARINE™ operates as a passive, non‑emissive sensing and acoustic‑dampening layer. It does not emit sonar or acoustic signals and therefore does not introduce new active acoustic signatures. Instead, VISL‑MARINE™ significantly enhances stealth‑compliancy through passive acoustic dampening, vibration isolation and reduction of hull‑borne noise signatures, contributing to a quieter and more discreet acoustic profile for the vessel.

VISL Monitoring Unit (VMU)

The VISL Monitoring Unit (VMU) is the hardware interface for VISL™. It provides power, signal conditioning, firmware logic and secure telemetry to the Vitium Analytics Platform.

Analytics Platform

The Vitium Analytics Platform provides anomaly detection, trend analysis, dashboards, alerts and integration with partner systems.

Certification Roadmap

VISL™ follows a multi‑phase certification pathway covering fire, electrical, environmental and sector‑specific standards. The VISL™ product family, including VISL‑MARINE™, is engineered to align with relevant regulatory and industry frameworks while maintaining a clear, auditable pathway toward formal certification in each target sector.
