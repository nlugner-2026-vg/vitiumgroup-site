VISL™ — Vitium Intelligent Safety Layer

The world’s first intelligent safety layer for thermal, moisture, vibration and structural sensing.

VISL™ is a patent‑pending multi‑modal sensing material designed for caravans, marine, medical, battery logistics, Electric Vehicle (EV) packs, Ministry of Defence (MOD) vehicles, aerospace, infrastructure and space systems.

What VISL™ Is

VISL™ is a passive, sector‑agnostic sensing layer that detects early‑stage failure conditions long before they become operational hazards. It integrates seamlessly with the VISL Monitoring Unit (VMU) and the Vitium Analytics Platform.

VISL Sensing Modes

  • Thermal sensing — early‑fire and runaway detection
  • Moisture sensing — ingress, leaks, humidity
  • Vibration sensing — fatigue, instability, imbalance
  • Pressure / tension sensing — deformation, load stress
  • Structural sensing — cracks, delamination, impact

VISL‑SLEEVE™ — Underground Pipe Safety Sleeve

VISL‑SLEEVE™ is a patent‑pending sensing sleeve designed for underground water pipes, providing early detection of moisture ingress, thermal anomalies, vibration, deformation and structural stress.

  • Moisture sensing — leak, ingress, saturation
  • Thermal sensing — freeze, overheating, abnormal gradients
  • Vibration sensing — instability, external disturbance
  • Structural sensing — deformation, stress, impact

Suitable for water mains, service pipes, distribution pipes, deep urban assets, river crossings and critical buried infrastructure.

VISL‑MARINE™ — Marine Sensing and Acoustic‑Dampening Layer

VISL‑MARINE™ is a patent‑pending multi‑modal sensing and acoustic‑dampening layer designed for marine vessels and underwater assets, including submarines.

  • Moisture sensing — ingress, leaks, condensation
  • Thermal sensing — overheating, cold‑shock, abnormal gradients
  • Vibration sensing — instability, engine imbalance, hull resonance
  • Structural sensing — impact, stress, deformation
  • Acoustic dampening — noise reduction, vibration isolation, resonance control

VISL‑MARINE™ operates as a passive, non‑emissive sensing and acoustic‑dampening layer, enhancing stealth‑compliancy.
