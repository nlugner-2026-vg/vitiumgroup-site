Vitium Group – Learn More

Strategic advisory, infrastructure resilience and VERITAS truth-layer services across national verticals.

Historical Data Scanning and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) Extraction

Digitising handwritten notes, legacy logs, books, archives and historical work orders. Text from photos, site images and field captures can be extracted and merged into existing data records. Legacy audio files (WAV, MP3 and similar formats) can also be transcribed into structured text.

All data can be enriched with historical weather context and Global Positioning System (GPS) longitude and latitude for new field inputs, and aligned to National Underground Asset Register (NUAR)-compliant and Ordnance Survey (OS) / Geographic Information System (GIS) data structures — providing a single source of immutable truth across all Vitium Group verticals.

Foundational truth layer

VERITAS – Strategic Advisory and Signals

Evidence-grade advisory built on immutable truth data. VERITAS ingests historical records, live signals and field intelligence to support councils, utilities, national infrastructure and central government with aligned, defensible decision-making.

Signals and governance

IRaaS – Infrastructure Resilience as a Service

Continuous resilience assessment for critical infrastructure — utilities, transport, communications and public assets. Combines historical data, live monitoring and field inspections to identify weak points, prioritise interventions and support long-term asset health.

Resilience services

IRaaS-WATER – Water Resilience, Harvesting and Storage

Water harvesting, storage, treatment and distribution across residential, commercial, industrial, agricultural and municipal scales. Integrates site data, historical performance and weather patterns to support secure, resilient water systems.

Water resilience

Highways and Bridges – National Road Infrastructure and Structural Resilience

Digitising historical bridge logs, inspection sheets, handwritten notes and work orders. Extracted data is merged into existing asset records, enriched with historical weather context and Global Positioning System (GPS) longitude and latitude for new field inspections. Fully aligned with National Underground Asset Register (NUAR) and Ordnance Survey (OS) / Geographic Information System (GIS) data structures. Supports proactive bridge health monitoring — addressing the emerging national issue often described as the “next pothole epidemic”.

Roads and structures

Telecommunications and Fibre – Network Mapping, Resilience and Field Intelligence

Digitising legacy fibre plans, duct maps, jointing records, pole logs and handwritten field notes. Extracted data is merged into existing network records, enriched with weather and Global Positioning System (GPS) longitude and latitude, and aligned to National Underground Asset Register (NUAR) and Ordnance Survey (OS) / Geographic Information System (GIS) standards. Provides a unified, immutable truth layer for fibre, broadband, fifth generation (5G) and critical communications infrastructure.

Communications infrastructure
