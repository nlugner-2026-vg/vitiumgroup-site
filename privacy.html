Privacy Policy

Vitium Group operates governed digital infrastructure with strict privacy, evidence integrity, and data‑handling discipline.

This policy outlines how information is handled across VERITAS™, VISL™, and AMP8‑aligned systems.

Data Principles

  • Integrity — all data is handled with evidence‑grade discipline.
  • Accountability — governed workflows ensure traceability and provenance.
  • Minimalism — only essential operational data is processed.
  • Security — multi‑layer protection across physical, telemetry, and digital systems.

Information We Collect

Vitium Group collects operational data required for governed workflows, sensing systems, and programme execution. This may include:

  • Operational workflow data (Admin / Office / Field / Unified)
  • VISL™ telemetry and sensing outputs
  • VERITAS™ evidence capture metadata
  • Programme reporting and oversight information

How Data Is Used

Data is used strictly for operational, governance, and safety purposes, including:

  • Evidence governance and chain‑of‑custody
  • Anomaly detection and sensing analysis
  • Operational reporting and oversight
  • Workflow execution and programme alignment

Data Protection

Vitium Group employs multi‑layer protection across physical systems, telemetry pathways, and governed digital infrastructure. All data is secured using industry‑grade encryption and access‑controlled workflows.

Third‑Party Alignment

Partners operating within AMP8 must adhere to Vitium Group’s governed data‑handling requirements. No data is shared externally without strict governance and operational justification.
