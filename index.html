Strategic advisory, infrastructure resilience and VERITAS truth‑layer services across national verticals.
Historical Data Scanning & OCR Extraction
Digitising handwritten notes, legacy logs, books, archives and historical work orders. Text from photos, site images and field captures can be extracted and merged into existing data records. Legacy audio files (WAV, MP3, etc.) can also be transcribed into structured text. All data can be enriched with historical weather context and GPS long/lat for new field inputs, and aligned to NUAR‑compliant and OS/GIS (Geographic Information System) data structures — providing a single source of immutable truth across all Vitium Group verticals.
Foundational truth layer
VERITAS – Strategic Advisory & Signals
Evidence‑grade advisory built on immutable truth data. VERITAS ingests historical records, live signals and field intelligence to support councils, utilities, national infrastructure and central government with aligned, defensible decision‑making.
Signals & governance
IRaaS – Infrastructure Resilience as a Service
Continuous resilience assessment for critical infrastructure — utilities, transport, communications and public assets. Combines historical data, live monitoring and field inspections to identify weak points, prioritise interventions and support long‑term asset health.
Resilience services
IRaaS‑WATER – Water Resilience / Harvesting / Storage
Water harvesting, storage, treatment and distribution across residential, commercial, industrial, agricultural and municipal scales. Integrates site data, historical performance and weather patterns to support secure, resilient water systems.
Water resilience
Highways & Bridges – National Road Infrastructure & Structural Resilience
Digitising historical bridge logs, inspection sheets, handwritten notes and work orders. Extracted data is merged into existing asset records, enriched with historical weather context and GPS long/lat for new field inspections. Fully aligned with NUAR and OS/GIS (Geographic Information System) data structures. Supports proactive bridge health monitoring — addressing the emerging national issue often described as the “next pothole epidemic”.
Roads & structures
Telecoms & Fibre – Network Mapping, Resilience & Field Intelligence
Digitising legacy fibre plans, duct maps, jointing records, pole logs and handwritten field notes. Extracted data is merged into existing network records, enriched with weather and GPS long/lat, and aligned to NUAR and OS/GIS (Geographic Information System) standards. Provides a unified, immutable truth layer for fibre, broadband, 5G and critical communications infrastructure.
Communications infrastructure
