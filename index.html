VERITAS™ — Evidence Governance Platform

VERITAS™ is Vitium Group’s evidence governance platform, designed to protect truth in complex, high‑pressure environments. It provides a structured, immutable chain of custody for operational evidence.

  • Immutable evidence capture
  • Governed workflows
  • Admin / Office / Field / Unified roles
  • Provenance‑controlled event tracking

VISL™ — Intelligent Safety Layer

VISL™ is a patent‑pending multi‑modal sensing material for thermal, moisture, vibration, pressure, and structural anomaly detection across marine, EV, aerospace, logistics, MOD, infrastructure, and space systems.

  • Thermal sensing
  • Moisture sensing
  • Vibration sensing
  • Pressure / tension sensing
  • Structural sensing
