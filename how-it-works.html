How It Works

VERITAS™, VISL™, and AMP8 operate together as a governed, multi‑layer operational system.

This page explains how sensing, workflows, evidence governance, and operational alignment function across Vitium Group’s digital and physical infrastructure.

1. Sensing & Detection (VISL™)

VISL™ provides multi‑modal sensing across marine, EV, aerospace, defence, infrastructure, and space environments.

  • Thermal, optical, acoustic, and environmental sensing
  • VMU telemetry pathways
  • Anomaly detection
  • Fixed, mobile, and distributed sensing layers

2. Evidence Capture (VERITAS™)

Evidence enters the system through governed VERITAS™ workflows, ensuring integrity, provenance, and chain‑of‑custody.

  • Structured intake
  • Immutable metadata
  • Governed workflows
  • Role‑specific validation

3. Workflow Execution

Admin, Office, Field, and Unified roles execute governed workflows that maintain operational discipline and accountability.

  • Admin — governance, configuration, access control
  • Office — reporting, oversight, alignment
  • Field — evidence capture, sensing deployment
  • Unified — cross‑role operational visibility

4. AMP8 Architecture

AMP8 provides the multi‑layer architecture that connects sensing, workflows, evidence, telemetry, governance, and resilience systems.

  • Layered operational structure
  • Cross‑environment integration
  • Governed digital infrastructure
  • National‑scale resilience alignment

5. Reporting & Oversight

VERITAS™ generates structured reports that maintain clarity, accountability, and long‑term operational integrity.

  • Evidence reports
  • Workflow compliance
  • VISL™ sensing summaries
  • Governance audits
