Industry Solutions

Vitium Group supports sectors requiring governed evidence, intelligent sensing, and structured operational workflows.

Our platforms provide clarity, accountability, and anomaly detection across demanding environments.

Marine

VISL‑MARINE™ provides multi‑modal sensing and acoustic‑dampening for hulls, bulkheads, engine bays, battery compartments, and underwater assets.

EV & Battery Logistics

VISL™ enables early detection of thermal, moisture, vibration, and structural anomalies in EV packs, logistics containers, and battery transport systems.

MOD & Defence

VERITAS™ and VISL™ support defence environments requiring governed evidence, anomaly detection, and structured operational workflows.

Aerospace

VISL™ provides sensing capabilities for aerospace structures, thermal systems, vibration environments, and orbital assets.

Infrastructure

VISL‑SLEEVE™ enables moisture, thermal, vibration, and structural sensing for underground pipes, buried assets, and critical infrastructure.

Space Systems

VISL‑SPACE™ provides multi‑modal sensing for orbital structures, spacecraft systems, and high‑risk thermal and vibration environments.
