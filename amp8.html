AMP8 Architecture

AMP8 is Vitium Group’s multi‑layer operational architecture connecting sensing, evidence, workflows, telemetry, governance, and resilience systems.

It provides the structural backbone for VERITAS™, VISL™, FireWatch™, and all governed operational environments.

The Eight Layers

AMP8 consists of eight governed layers that unify physical systems, digital infrastructure, telemetry, evidence governance, and operational workflows.

  • Layer 1 — Physical Systems Machinery, infrastructure, sensing hardware, and operational environments.
  • Layer 2 — Telemetry VMU pathways, VISL™ sensing outputs, and multi‑modal data streams.
  • Layer 3 — Evidence Capture Governed VERITAS™ intake, provenance, and chain‑of‑custody.
  • Layer 4 — Workflows Admin, Office, Field, and Unified governed processes.
  • Layer 5 — Analytics Operational insights, anomaly detection, and structured reporting.
  • Layer 6 — Governance Rules, access control, auditability, and operational discipline.
  • Layer 7 — Integration Cross‑environment alignment across national resilience sectors.
  • Layer 8 — Resilience National‑scale readiness, FireWatch™, IRaaS, and critical infrastructure support.

How AMP8 Connects the Ecosystem

AMP8 ensures that every operational event — from sensing to reporting — follows governed, accountable, and structured pathways.

  • VISL™ sensing feeds telemetry into AMP8
  • VERITAS™ captures and governs evidence
  • Dashboards execute workflows across roles
  • FireWatch™ integrates into national resilience
  • IRaaS sectors operate within AMP8 alignment

National Resilience Alignment

AMP8 supports national‑scale resilience programmes across water, highways, telecoms, modular housing, SMR, and environmental sensing.
