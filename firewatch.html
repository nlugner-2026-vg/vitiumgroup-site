FireWatch™ — National Detection & Readiness Grid

FireWatch™ is Vitium Group’s fixed‑infrastructure detection layer for ignition, heat, fire‑risk, and environmental threat monitoring.

It operates as a distributed grid of pylons, sensors, and fixed installations, providing early‑stage detection and governed operational truth through VERITAS™.

Detection Capabilities

  • Ignition detection
  • Thermal‑risk monitoring
  • Environmental threat sensing
  • Long‑range VISL™ multi‑modal detection
  • Real‑time telemetry via VMU

Infrastructure Grid

FireWatch™ operates as a national‑scale grid of fixed installations, providing continuous monitoring across forests, rural land, infrastructure corridors, and high‑risk zones.

  • Pylon‑mounted sensing arrays
  • Ground‑level VISL™ nodes
  • High‑altitude thermal detection
  • Distributed VMU telemetry pathways

VERITAS™ Integration

All FireWatch™ events are governed through VERITAS™, ensuring evidence integrity, chain‑of‑custody, and structured operational workflows.

  • Governed alert pathways
  • Immutable provenance
  • Operational workflow alignment
  • Cross‑agency reporting

National Readiness

FireWatch™ provides early‑stage detection and readiness support for emergency services, forestry agencies, infrastructure operators, and national resilience programmes.
