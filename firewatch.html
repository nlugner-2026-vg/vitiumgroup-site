FireWatch™ — National Detection & Readiness Grid

FireWatch™ is Vitium Group’s fixed‑infrastructure detection layer for ignition, heat, fire‑risk and environmental threat monitoring.

It operates as a distributed grid of pylons, sensors and fixed installations, providing early‑stage detection and governed operational truth through Vitium Veritas™.

FireWatch Pylons

FireWatch pylons form the backbone of the detection grid. Each pylon hosts multi‑modal sensors, telemetry modules and Veritas‑linked assurance logic. They are deployed across critical zones, rural areas, industrial sites and national infrastructure.

  • Thermal detection arrays
  • Ignition signature monitoring
  • Air quality & particulate sensing
  • Environmental threat detection
  • Secure telemetry uplink

FireWatch Sensor Arrays

FireWatch sensor arrays provide continuous monitoring for early‑stage ignition, heat anomalies and environmental risk factors. They integrate with VISL™, SmartSkin™ and Flood‑Watch™ where required.

  • Heat & ignition sensors
  • Infrared & thermal gradient sensors
  • Atmospheric & particulate sensors
  • Humidity & saturation sensors
  • Environmental anomaly detection

FireWatch Grid Telemetry

All FireWatch pylons and sensors feed into a unified telemetry layer. Data is processed through Veritas™ for governed operational truth, ensuring accurate escalation pathways and readiness state reporting.

FireWatch‑1 Sheet

The FireWatch‑1 sheet defines the operational cycle, escalation triggers and assurance checkpoints. It is used for deployment planning, readiness tracking and incident logging across the FireWatch grid.

Flood‑Watch Integration

FireWatch™ pairs with Flood‑Watch™ to provide dual‑mode environmental readiness. FireWatch handles ignition and heat detection, while Flood‑Watch monitors water‑level, saturation and environmental threat conditions.

UK Readiness State

FireWatch™ contributes to the UK readiness state by providing governed, real‑time operational truth across fire‑risk and environmental threats. It supports local authorities, national resilience frameworks and critical infrastructure operators.

Veritas™ Assurance

All FireWatch™ data flows through Vitium Veritas™ for immutable evidence cycles, governed telemetry and operational truth. This ensures accuracy, compliance and readiness integrity.
