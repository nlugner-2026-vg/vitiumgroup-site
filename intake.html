Evidence Intake

Governed evidence intake for VERITAS™ workflows and AMP8‑aligned operational environments.

Intake is the first stage of the evidence lifecycle, ensuring structured capture, provenance integrity, and chain‑of‑custody enforcement.

Intake Workflow

All evidence enters the system through governed VERITAS™ intake workflows, ensuring:

  • Structured capture
  • Immutable metadata
  • Role‑specific validation
  • Provenance protection

Field Intake

Field users capture evidence directly from operational environments, including VISL™ sensing outputs, VMU telemetry, and physical observations.

  • On‑site capture
  • VISL™ integration
  • VMU‑linked metadata
  • Immediate chain‑of‑custody assignment

Office Intake

Office users validate, classify, and structure incoming evidence for reporting, governance, and operational alignment.

  • Classification
  • Governance checks
  • Workflow assignment
  • Reporting preparation

Admin Oversight

Admin users enforce intake rules, workflow boundaries, and governance requirements across all roles.

  • Workflow configuration
  • Access control
  • Governance enforcement
  • Audit trail verification

Unified Intake View

Unified users access cross‑role intake summaries, enabling multi‑environment operational clarity and AMP8 alignment.
