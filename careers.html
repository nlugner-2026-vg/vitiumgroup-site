Careers at Vitium Group

Join a disciplined, forward‑leaning organisation building evidence governance systems and intelligent sensing technologies.

Vitium Group recruits individuals who value clarity, structure, operational integrity, and long‑term accountability.

Our Culture

Vitium Group operates with precision and purpose. We build platforms that protect truth, enhance safety, and support organisations working in complex, high‑pressure environments.

We value disciplined thinking, structured workflows, and a commitment to operational excellence.

Roles & Opportunities

  • Software Engineering (Platform, Infrastructure, Telemetry)
  • Data Governance & Evidence Integrity
  • Field Operations & Deployment
  • Digital Infrastructure Workflow Design
  • VISL™ Sensing & Installation Specialists
  • Programme Reporting & Oversight
  • Operational Governance & Compliance

What We Look For

  • Structured, disciplined thinkers
  • Strong operational awareness
  • Commitment to truth, clarity, and accountability
  • Ability to work in governed, high‑integrity environments
  • Technical competence and willingness to learn

How to Apply

To apply for a role at Vitium Group, please contact our recruitment team through the Contact page.

Include your CV, relevant experience, and a short statement outlining your interest in evidence governance or intelligent sensing systems.
